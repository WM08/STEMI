Robot-spider

STEMI Hexapod is a robot that looks and walks like a spider, commanded by a smartphone. It’s walking algorithm emulates real walking and movement.

The mobile app takes advantage of the modern technology such as gyroscope in your smartphone.
This allows for the calculation of orientation and rotation of the robot's body.

  • Height change
  • Complex movements
  • 3 types of walking
  • Dance

Assemble the robot - learn STEM

Your robot will arrive in parts and pieces, with all the components you need. On top of that, you will receive access to high-quality video tutorials, covering everything – from 3D modeling, Electronics, Mathematics to programming a mobile application.

We made sure that STEMI can be built by everyone. All you need to do is to follow the instructions and connect the parts. You may need a calm hand, though.
